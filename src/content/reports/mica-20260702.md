## What Is Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA)

Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) is the EU's comprehensive crypto-asset regulation — covering issuance, trading, custody, and disclosure for crypto-assets not already governed by traditional financial services law. MiCA defines authorisation for crypto-asset service providers (CASPs), white-paper requirements, e-money token (EMT) and asset-referenced token (ART) rules, and transitional grandfathering through mid-2026. For the tokenized settlement layer, MiCA is the most fully specified regulatory perimeter globally — the rulebook that Circle, Coinbase EU, Bitstamp, and EU-facing issuers must navigate for cross-border dollar and euro settlement.
