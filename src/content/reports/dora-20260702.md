## What Is Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)

The Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA) is the EU regulation governing ICT risk management, incident reporting, and third-party oversight for financial entities — applying from 17 January 2025 across banking, securities, insurance, and crypto-asset service providers in scope. DORA requires registers of ICT contractual arrangements and establishes EU-wide oversight of critical ICT third-party providers (CTPPs). For the tokenized settlement layer, DORA is the operational-resilience waist: cloud custodians, market-data vendors, and blockchain infrastructure providers serving EU CASPs and broker-dealers must meet harmonised ICT security and resilience standards.
